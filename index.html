What makes a strong password?

Long and complex

A password that is at least 15 characters long and includes a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Unique

A password that is not used for any other accounts and is not easily guessable based on personal information.

Two-factor authentication

A password that is used in combination with another form of authentication, such as a fingerprint or a security token.

Random

A password that is generated by a password manager or random password generator, rather than being something that can be easily guessed.

Phrase-based

A password that is a phrase or sentence that is easy to remember, but difficult for others to guess.

Regularly rotated

A password that is changed regularly to reduce the risk of it being compromised.
